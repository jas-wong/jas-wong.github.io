Greetings.

I am a Ph.D. student interested in fluid-structure interactions, active and passive control of flow-induced vibration, and computational and experimental methods for studying aeroelastic phenomena.

More Information

Currently, my research investigates the influence of avian wing morphing on the material properties of the soft tissue as well as the multi-element, feathered flight surface, and how these changes in mechanical properties will affect the wing's aeroelastic response during flight. I use a combination of experimental materials testing and vibrometry and computational fluid-structure interaction simulations.
